    About Me

    Omid Mahramzadeh, professionally known as PrOmid, is an award-winning composer, electronic music producer, sound engineer, artistic director, and music therapist with more than two decades of international experience in music and multimedia production. Holding a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, a Master's degree in Electronic Music, and a professional specialization in Music Therapy, PrOmid combines artistic creativity with technical expertise to create immersive sonic experiences that bridge electronic music, orchestral composition, ambient sound design, and wellness-focused audio concepts. Since 2008, he has collaborated with the renowned Buddha-Bar ecosystem, contributing to luxury music productions and international projects. Throughout his career, he has released more than 50 albums across various electronic music genres through respected international record labels and has had over 200 compositions featured in globally recognized compilations. As a composer and producer, PrOmid has worked with acclaimed musicians, performers, filmmakers, and creative organizations worldwide. His work spans electronic music, film scoring, live performances, immersive entertainment experiences, and innovative music therapy programs designed to enhance focus, relaxation, emotional balance, and personal well-being. His artistic achievements have been recognized by several prestigious festivals and institutions, including honors from the Fajr International Music Festival, the Fajr Film Festival, and the Canadian International Film Festival. He has also received recognition for his film music contributions from international film festivals in Canada and Toronto. PrOmid is an official member of the Iranian House of Music and a member of the Fédération Internationale des Musiciens (FIM) in France. His work is characterized by a distinctive fusion of electronic music, cinematic storytelling, cultural influences, and contemporary sound innovation. Today, PrOmid continues to develop international music projects, luxury entertainment concepts, film scores, live performances, and transformative music experiences that connect audiences through emotion, technology, and artistic excellence.
